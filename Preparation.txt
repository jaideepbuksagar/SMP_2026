Pretext for the course Simulation of Mechanical Processes

The course mainly focuses on 3 aspects:
- Numerical methods for solving ODEs & PDEs
- Monte Carlo Simulations
- Discrete Element Models (FEA)

Theories required along the duratin of the course
1. Statistics
2. Probability Theory
3. ODE & PDE (Real & Complex Analysis)
4. Solid Mechanics
5. Collision kinematics
6. Python or MATLAB
